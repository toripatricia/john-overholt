BIOGRAPHY

JOHN OVERHOLT, TENOR, hails from Newport, Tennessee. John completed his graduate studies at the prestigious University of Cincinnati College-Conservatory of Music, and his undergraduate degree at East Tennessee State University. He has performed with the Cincinnati Opera Chorus for several years, and gained national attention for his performance as Justice Antonin Scalia in the world premiere of Derrick Wang’s Scalia/Ginsburg at the Castleton Festival in 2015, which was featured on National Public Radio. John recently performed in Knoxville Opera’s production of Verdi’s Falstaff where he played Dr. Caius and John performed Gastone in Knoxville Opera’s La Traviata. John’s passion for music and commitment to excellence in his craft have earned him numerous accolades and opportunities to perform throughout the United States.
